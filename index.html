Risks Detection and Assessment

  • Three main types of skin cancer:

    ✔ Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC):
    → Origin: Arises from basal cells,located in the lower part of the epidermis(the outer layer of the skin).
    → Characteristics: BCC is the most common type of skin cancer,usually appears as a flesh-colored or pinkish bump on the skin.


    ✔ Squamous Cell Carcinoma (SCC):
    → Origin: Develops in the squamous cells,found in the upper layers of the epidermis.
    → Characteristics: SCC is the second most common type of skin cancer,presents as a firm, red nodule or a flat sore with a scaly crust.


    ✔ Melanoma:
    → Origin: Arises from melanocytes, the pigment-producing cells in the skin.
    → Characteristics: Melanoma is less common than BCC and SCC but is more aggressive.
